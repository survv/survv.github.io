Seeking New Oceans, Facing My Fears

What are you scared of? To me, it's the unknown. Not knowing if the path you choose is the right one, or if you're walking on a path that will lead you to despair. But if you don't make a move, will you ever reach your destination, or are you stripping yourself from all the possibilities this life holds for you? So, I'll face my fears and put one foot infront of the other until my legs give in.
Because that's the only direction that I know: forward.

Let's figure out what this blog will be about, shall we?

I've recently started learning Swift, HTML, CSS and will soon start with JavaScript and Python.
 To be quite frank with you, I never considered the possibility, that I'd someday be interested in programming - but here I am.

Let's see where this path will lead us.
